Women for Freedom is a Guarantee Limited Company initiated by a collective​e of individuals passionate about the safety and empowerment of women in Sri Lanka.

Established with the aim of empowering women, and fighting to minimize gender-based violence against both women and children of our country, in order to create a safer Sri Lanka, Women for Freedom is dedicated to creating community-based safety networks, and raising awareness.

Why we do what we do

Domestic violence is on the rise in Sri Lanka and with each passing day, the cases get more severe and brutal. When domestic violence is prevalent within the family it breaks down the family unit, thus compromising all the value systems and ultimately creating an unsafe environment for the children. With parents being more preoccupied with their own issues, their children are more susceptible to child abuse, drug addictions and psychological disorders, which does not only worsen the family’s problems, but also plays as a direct threat to society. 
It is therefore evident that the family unit is the root of the good, the bad, and everything in between that exists within society, which is why Women for Freedom aims to target the root causes of these issues, in an attempt to create a better, safer, and a more empowered Sri Lanka for our women and children.

what we do

Empow​erment

Empower women and children. Aiding women to understand their value, making them independent so that they can become self-sustaining 

Awareness

Conducting awareness workshops throughout the country for women, children and State Actors who are directly involved in related affairs.

Holistic Justice

Providing le​gal aid, safe housing and follow-up mental health assistance to ensure that survivors receive holistic justice.

How we do it

Workshops conducted for State Actors aid them to sympathize and empathize more with the victims of domestic violence, thereby motivating them to provide the necessary redress speedily.

Workshops also focus on identifying abusive relationships, and guide women on when they should leave an unfavorable situation.
Workshops aimed at school children to raise awareness on gender inequality and abolishing gender roles.


Ensuring a victim centered holistic justice system through safe housing as well as legal aid and follow up mental health assistance

Conducting skill development that aid women to understand their value, enabling them to be more independent and self-sustaining

Child Protection Force (Guarantee Limited) was launched in September 2017. This is a brainchild of Attorney-at-Law Milani Salpitikorala, who has a vision to create a Safe Sri Lanka for her children.Child Protection Force consists of members and employees who have a passion for Child Rights Law and would work effortlessly towards a change to the entire system for Child Protection in Sri Lanka.

Power of Play is the result of a bold, entrepreneurial spirit and a passion for performing arts.
